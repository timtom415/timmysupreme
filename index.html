About

Timothy Ng

Between San Francisco (SF) and Boston (BOS). I'm a software engineer who ships full-stack systems with an eye toward performance, readability, and how things behave under failure.

Day to day I'm building APIs, frontend surfaces, and the glue between them—always trying to strip work down to what's essential and dependable.

I'm drawn to infra-minded product work, OSS-adjacent tooling, and collaborations where design and engineering stay in tight feedback loops.

Right now I'm focused on small teams, calm interfaces, and code that survives its first outage.
